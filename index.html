About Me

Hi, I'm Austin!

I've spent the last 13 years working across multiple roles in logistics, from account manager to sales, only to learn that the part I most enjoy is engineering solutions for my customers.

I believe my experience in an incredibly fast paced industry with a focus on forward thinking and creative problem solving is a skillset that can easily be applied to a more techincal position. Having spent such a long time communicating with contacts all across the corporate ladder, from baseline users, all the way to high level corporate positions such as presidents and CEOs is not something provided by most developers with similar experience.

Out of the office, I still dream of designing, but for my hobbies: I play tons of games, both digital and tabletop, I dabble in guitar, and I am an avid (if unsuccessful) gardener. I also go snorkeling with my favorite marine biologist when the weather allows!
